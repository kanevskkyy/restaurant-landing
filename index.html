Our History

Like any other unique place, we have our own special story. The idea for the restaurant came to the founders unexpectedly. While walking through the woods, the founder of our restaurant found himself stranded hundreds of kilometers from the nearest town. Far from civilization and communication, they had to quickly set up a simple living situation for themselves, forage for food, and cook their own meals.
